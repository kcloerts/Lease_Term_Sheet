COMMERCIAL LEASE AGREEMENT

This Lease Agreement ("Lease") is entered into as of January 1, 2024, by and between:

LANDLORD: Metropolitan Property Group, LLC
123 Main Street, Suite 500
New York, NY 10001

TENANT: TechStart Innovations, Inc.
456 Broadway Avenue
New York, NY 10002

RECITALS

WHEREAS, Landlord is the owner of certain commercial property located at 789 Commerce Plaza, New York, NY 10003; and

WHEREAS, Tenant desires to lease a portion of the property for the operation of a technology consulting business.

NOW, THEREFORE, in consideration of the mutual covenants and agreements herein contained, the parties agree as follows:

ARTICLE 1 - PREMISES

1.1 The Landlord hereby leases to Tenant and Tenant hereby leases from Landlord, Suite 1200, consisting of approximately 5,000 rentable square feet located on the 12th floor of the building at 789 Commerce Plaza, New York, NY 10003 (the "Premises").

1.2 The Premises shall be used exclusively for general office use and technology consulting services.

ARTICLE 2 - TERM

2.1 The term of this Lease shall be five (5) years, commencing on February 1, 2024 (the "Commencement Date") and expiring on January 31, 2029 (the "Expiration Date").

2.2 Tenant shall have two (2) options to extend the Lease for additional five-year periods, provided Tenant is not in default and provides written notice to Landlord at least 180 days prior to the expiration of the then-current term.

ARTICLE 3 - BASE RENT

3.1 Tenant shall pay to Landlord annual base rent as follows:

    Years 1-2: $250,000 per year ($20,833.33 per month)
    Years 3-4: $262,500 per year ($21,875.00 per month)
    Year 5: $275,625 per year ($22,968.75 per month)

3.2 Base rent increases by 5% every two years.

3.3 All rent payments shall be due on the first day of each month without demand, deduction, or offset.

ARTICLE 4 - ADDITIONAL CHARGES

4.1 Operating Expenses: Tenant shall pay its proportionate share (5.5% of total building) of all operating expenses, including but not limited to maintenance, repairs, insurance, and management fees.

4.2 Property Taxes: Tenant shall pay its proportionate share of all real estate taxes and assessments levied against the property.

4.3 Utilities: Tenant shall be responsible for and shall pay all costs for electricity, gas, water, telephone, internet, and other utilities consumed in the Premises.

4.4 Common Area Maintenance (CAM): Tenant shall pay $2.50 per square foot annually for common area maintenance, subject to annual adjustment based on actual costs.

ARTICLE 5 - SECURITY DEPOSIT

5.1 Upon execution of this Lease, Tenant shall deposit with Landlord the sum of $62,500.00 (three months' base rent) as a security deposit.

5.2 The security deposit shall be held by Landlord and returned to Tenant within thirty (30) days after the expiration or termination of this Lease, less any deductions for damages or unpaid rent.

ARTICLE 6 - TENANT IMPROVEMENTS

6.1 Landlord shall provide Tenant with a tenant improvement allowance of $100,000 ($20 per square foot) to be used for improvements to the Premises.

6.2 Tenant shall have sixty (60) days from the Commencement Date to complete all tenant improvements.

6.3 All improvements shall be subject to Landlord's prior written approval.

ARTICLE 7 - PARKING

7.1 Tenant shall be entitled to ten (10) unreserved parking spaces in the building's parking garage at no additional charge.

7.2 Tenant may request up to five (5) additional reserved parking spaces at a rate of $300 per space per month.

ARTICLE 8 - INSURANCE AND INDEMNITY

8.1 Tenant shall maintain comprehensive general liability insurance with minimum coverage of $2,000,000 per occurrence and $5,000,000 aggregate.

8.2 Tenant shall maintain property insurance covering all personal property and improvements in the Premises.

ARTICLE 9 - MAINTENANCE AND REPAIRS

9.1 Landlord shall maintain the building structure, roof, exterior walls, and common areas.

9.2 Tenant shall maintain the interior of the Premises, including all fixtures, equipment, and improvements installed by Tenant.

ARTICLE 10 - DEFAULT AND REMEDIES

10.1 In the event of default by Tenant, Landlord may terminate this Lease and pursue all remedies available at law or in equity.

10.2 Default includes failure to pay rent within ten (10) days of due date, violation of any lease term, or abandonment of the Premises.

ARTICLE 11 - ASSIGNMENT AND SUBLETTING

11.1 Tenant shall not assign this Lease or sublet the Premises without prior written consent of Landlord, which consent shall not be unreasonably withheld.

ARTICLE 12 - BROKERS

12.1 Landlord's broker: Empire Realty Partners, LLC
12.2 Tenant's broker: Downtown Commercial Advisors

12.3 Landlord shall pay all brokerage commissions in accordance with separate agreements.

ARTICLE 13 - SPECIAL PROVISIONS

13.1 Tenant shall have the right of first refusal on Suite 1210 (adjacent suite) should it become available during the lease term.

13.2 Landlord agrees to complete HVAC system upgrades to the 12th floor prior to Commencement Date at Landlord's expense.

13.3 Tenant may install signage in the building lobby and on the 12th floor directory, subject to Landlord's approval of design and placement.

13.4 Tenant shall have 24/7 access to the Premises and building.
